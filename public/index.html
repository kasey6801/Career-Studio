How this works

Career Positioning Studio uses large language models to analyse your career evidence and generate four tailored documents in parallel.

  • LinkedIn Update Plan — new headline options, rewritten About section, experience bullet rewrites, top skills to pin, and a hygiene checklist.
  • Master CV — ATS-safe, reverse-chronological, with [FILL: …] placeholders for missing data. The model never invents employers, dates, or certifications.
  • Professional Development Plan — a 6–12 month certification roadmap, hands-on projects, networking milestones, and curated learning resources.
  • 10 Target Roles — with fit scores, gap analysis, and ready-to-paste LinkedIn and Indeed search queries.

Your API key is sent with each request to the LLM provider and is never stored on any server. All generation happens in your browser session.

Works with any OpenAI-compatible provider: Anthropic, OpenAI, Together AI, Mistral, Ollama (local), LM Studio, and more.
